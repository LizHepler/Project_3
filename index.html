EMS Calls for Opioid Emergencies

This project shows the graphical visualization of emergency call made for opioids in the city of Tempe, AZ. The data is collected from the website, Data.gov. This has the data from 2017 to 2024.

MAP Tab: The data is analyzed to map the locations that the incident happened, giving date, age and gender details.

CHARTS Tab: Charts are derived for all the years:

1. Bar chart is created by month to analyze if any particular season has maximum calls. The data shows that maximum calls were made during the month of July. Probably due to summer.

2. Pie Chart is created by age range in 5yr span. More people were used between 25 to 34 years. Probably due to stress?

3. Line Chart is created by gender. The data shows that more men are in emergency than women.
